Admissions: Your Journey Starts Here

Join a selective, inclusive community of innovators.

Application Process

  1. Submit online registration form.
  2. Provide transcripts and test scores.
  3. Attend virtual or in-person interview.
  4. Await admission decision within 4 weeks.

Requirements

  • High school GPA 3.2+
  • SAT/ACT (optional for international students)
  • 500-word essay on your engineering passion
  • Two recommendation letters

Deadlines

Fall Intake: March 15 | Spring Intake: October 15

Tuition & Fees

Item Cost (USD)
Tuition (per semester) 12,000
Technology Fee 600
Housing (optional) 6,000
